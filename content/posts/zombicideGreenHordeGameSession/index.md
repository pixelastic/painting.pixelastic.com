We played two Zombicide sessions in May and December 2023 on a fully 3D terrain I built. Every square is a foam tile, every wall and door I crafted myself. These are just some photos from the games. I think we lost the first one and won the second.

Playing on a 3D board like this is really enjoyable. You get the feeling of zombie hordes actually flooding the streets. I'm very happy I built all of this because Zombicide is already a great game, and with 3D scenery it's even better.
